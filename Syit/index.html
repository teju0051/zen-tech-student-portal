    Info

    Welcome to the FY-IT 2024–2027 Leaderboard! This leaderboard is designed to showcase the outstanding academic and extracurricular performances of our students, updated semester-wise.

    Each season represents one academic semester, and the rankings are based on the internal evaluation data provided by the Exam Department. The scores are calculated using a weighted average of unit tests, assignments, attendance, and participation in department-level activities.

    🏅 Bonus Points: Students can earn bonus points through department-level competitions, workshops, and hackathons. These will be verified and approved by faculty before being added to the leaderboard.

    📆 Leaderboard gets refreshed at the end of each semester after final internal data review. Keep checking for updates and new recognitions!

    Note: If you notice any discrepancies or have questions regarding the rankings, please contact the Exam Department with your concern and supporting documents.
